Request For Production No. 1

All DOCUMENTS reflecting DEFENDANT's policies and procedures regarding meal periods for non-exempt employees during the CLASS PERIOD.

Response to Request For Production No. 1

Defendant objects to this Request on the grounds that it is overbroad, unduly burdensome, and seeks information that is neither relevant nor proportional to the needs of the case. Defendant further objects to the extent this Request seeks documents protected by the attorney-client privilege, work product doctrine, or any other applicable privilege. Defendant objects to the extent this Request seeks documents not in Defendant's possession, custody, or control. Defendant objects to the definition of "Class Period" as overbroad and not reflective of the actual putative class period alleged in the Complaint.

Subject to and without waiving the foregoing objections, Defendant will produce non-privileged policies and procedures relating to meal periods for non-exempt employees in California during the Class Period (January 1, 2020 through December 31, 2024), to the extent such documents exist and are in Defendant's possession, custody, or control.
